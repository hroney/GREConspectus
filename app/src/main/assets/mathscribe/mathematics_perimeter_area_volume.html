$●$ Perimeter, Area and Volume

Perimeter of circle$P=2πr$
Area of circle$A=πr^2$
Surface area of sphere$^a$$A=4πR^2$
Volume of sphere$V={4}/{3}πR^3$
Perimeter of ellipse$^b$$P=4aE({π}/{2},e)$ $≈2π√{{a^2+b^2}/{2}}$
Area of ellipse$A=πab$
Volume of ellipsoid$^c$$V=4π{abc}/{3}$
Surface area of cylinder$A=2πr(h+r)$
Volume of cylinder$V=πr^2h$
Area of circular cone$^d$$A=πrl$
Volume of cone or pyramid$V=A_b{h}/{3}$
Surface are of torus$A=π^2(r_1+r_2)(r_2-r_1)$
Volume of torus$V={π^2}/{4}(r_2^2+r_1^2)(r_2-r_1)$
Area$^d$ of spherical cap, depth $d$$V=πd^2\(R-{d}/{3}\)$
Solid angle of a circle from a point on its axis, $z$ from centre $Ω=2π\[1-{z}/{√{z^2+r^2}}\]$ $=2π(1-\cos α)$

$^{a,b,c}$

$^a$ Sphere defined by $x^2+y^2+z^2=R^2$.

$^b$ The approximation is exact when $e=0$ and $e≈0.91$, giving a maximum error of $11%$ at $e=1$.

$^c$ Ellipsoid defined by ${x^2}/{a^2}+{y^2}/{b^2}+{z^2}/{c^2}=1$.

$^d$ Curved surface only.

where

$$P$$perimeter$$a$$semi-major axis
$$r$$radius$$b$$semi-major axis
$$A$$area$$E$$elliptic integral of the second kind
$$R$$sphere radius$$e$$eccentricity $(=1-{b^2}/{c^2})$
$$c$$third semi-axis$$h$$height
$$l$$slant height$$A_b$$base area
$$r_1$$inner radius$$r_2$$outer radius
