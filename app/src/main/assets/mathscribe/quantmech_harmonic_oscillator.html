$●$ Harmonic oscillator

Schrodinger equation$-{ħ^2}/{2m}{∂^2ψ_n}/{∂x^2}+1/2mω^2x^2ψ_n=E_nψ_n$
Energy levels$^a$$E_n=(n+1/2)ħω$
Eigen- functions$ψ_n={H_n(x\text"/"a)\exp{[-x^2\text"/"(2a^2)]}}/{(n!2^naπ^{1\text"/"2})^{1\text"/"2}}$
where $a=({ħ}/{mω})^{1\text"/"2}$
Hermite polynomials$H_0(y)=1$, $H_1(y)=2y$, $H_2(y)=4y^2-2$
$H_{n+1}(y)=2yH_n(y)-2nH_{n-1}(y)$

$^a$ $E_0$ is the zero-point energy of the oscillator.

where

$ħ$$\text"(Planck constant)/"(2π)$$m$mass
$x$displacement$ψ_n$$n$th eigenfunction
$n$integer $≥0$$ω$angular frequency
$E_n$total energy in $n$th state$H_n$Hermite polynomials
