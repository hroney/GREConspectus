LCR circuits

$●$ LCR definitions

Current$I={dQ}/{dt}$
Ohm's law$V=IR$
Ohm's law (field form)$\bo J=σ\bo E$
Resistivity$ρ={1}/{σ}={RA}/{l}$
Capacitance$C={Q}/{V}$
Current through capacitor$I=C{dV}/{dt}$
Self-inductance$L={Φ}/{I}$
Voltage across inductor$V=-L{dI}/{dt}$
Mutual inductance$L_{12}={Φ_1}/{I_2}=L_{21}$
Coefficient of coupling$|L_{12}|=k√{L_1L_2}$
Linked magnetic flux through a coil$Φ=Nφ$
