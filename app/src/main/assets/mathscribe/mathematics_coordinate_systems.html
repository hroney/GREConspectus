$●$ Gradient

Rectangular coordinates: $\bo ∇f={∂f}/{∂x}\bo x↖{→}+ {∂f}/{∂y}\bo y↖{→}+{∂f}/{∂z}\bo z↖{→}$
Cylindrical coordinates: $\bo ∇f={∂f}/{∂ρ}\bo ρ↖{→}+ {1}/{ρ}{∂f}/{∂φ}\bo φ↖{→}+{∂f}/{∂z}\bo z↖{→}$
Spherical polar coordinates: $\bo ∇f={∂f}/{∂r}\bo r↖{→}+ {1}/{r}{∂f}/{∂θ}\bo θ↖{→}+{1}/{r\sin θ}{∂f}/{∂φ}\bo φ↖{→}$
General orthogonal coordinates: $\bo ∇f={\bo q_{1}↖{→}}/{h_1}{∂f}/{∂q_1} \bo q_{1}↖{→}+{\bo q_{2}↖{→}}/{h_2}{∂f}/{∂q_2}\bo q_{2}↖{→}+ {\bo q_{3}↖{→}}/{h_3}{∂f}/{∂q_3}\bo q_{3}↖{→}$

where

$f$ is scalar field, $∇$ is gradient, $→$ unit vector, $q_i$ and $h_i$ basis and metric element respectively
