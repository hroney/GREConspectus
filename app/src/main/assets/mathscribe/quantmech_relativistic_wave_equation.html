High energy and nuclear physics

$●$ Relativistic wave equations$^a$

Klein-Gordon equation (massive, spin zero particles)$(∇^2-m^2)\ψ={∂^2\ψ}/{∂t^2}$
Weyl equations (massive, spin $1\text"/"2$ particles)${∂\Ψ}/{∂t}=±\(σ_x{∂\Ψ}/{∂x}+σ_y{∂\Ψ}/{∂y}+σ_z{∂\Ψ}/{∂z}\)$
Dirac equation (massive, spin 1$\text"/"2$ particles)$(\iγ^μ∂μ-m)\Ψ=0$
where $∂μ=\({∂}/{∂t},{∂}/{∂x},{∂}/{∂y},{∂}/{∂z}\)$
$(γ^0)^2={\bo 1}_4$; $(γ^1)^2=(γ^2)^2=(γ^3)^2=-{\bo 1}_4$

$^a$ Written in normal coordinates $c=ħ=1$.

where

$\ψ$wave function$m$particle mass
$t$time$Ψ$spinor wavefunction
$σ$Pauli spin matrices$\i$$\i^2=-1$
$1_n$$n×n$ unit matrix$γ^μ$Dirac matrices:
$γ^0=(\table 1_2, 0; 0, -1_2)$$γ^i=(\table 0,σ_i; -σ_i, 0) $
