$●$ Differential equations

Laplace $\bo ∇^2 f=0$
Diffusion$^a$ ${∂f}/{∂t} =D\bo ∇^2f$
Helmholtz $\bo ∇^2f + α^2f=0$
Wave $\bo ∇^2f ={1}/{c^2}{∂^2 f}/{∂t^2}$
Legendre ${d}/{dx}\[(1-x^2){dy}/{dx}\]+l(l+1)y=0$
Associated Legendre ${d}/{dx}\[(1-x^2){dy}/{dx}\]$ $+\[l(l+1)-{m^2}/{1-x^2}\]y=0$
Bessel $x^2{d^2y}/{dx^2}+x{dy}/{dx}+(x^2-m^2)y=0$
Hermite ${d^2y}/{dx^2}-2x{dy}/{dx}+2 α y=0$
Laguerre $x{d^2y}/{dx^2}+(1-x){dy}/{dx}+ α y=0$
Associated Laguerre $x{d^2y}/{dx^2}+(1+k-x){dy}/{dx}+ α y=0$
Chebyshev $\(1-x^2\){d^2y}/{dx^2}-x{dy}/{dx}+n^2y=0$
Euler (or Cauchy) $x^2{d^2y}/{dx^2}+ax{dy}/{dx}+by=f(x)$
Bernoulli ${dy}/{dx}+p(x)y=q(x)y^a$
Airy ${d^2y}/{dx^2}=xy$

$^{a,b,c}$

$^a$ Also known as the "conduction equation". For thermal conduction, $f≡T$ and $D$, the thermal diffusivity, $D≡κ≡{λ}/{ρc_p}$, where $T$ is the temperature distribution, $λ$ the thermal conductivity, $ρ$ the density, and $c_p$ the specific heat capacity of the material.

where

$$f$$$f(x,y,z)$$$c$$wave speed
$$D$$diffusion coefficient$$l$$integer
$$α$$constant$$m$$integer
