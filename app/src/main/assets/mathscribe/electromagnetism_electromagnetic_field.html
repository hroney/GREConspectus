Electric fields$^a$

Uniformly charged sphere, radius $a$, charge $q$ $\bo E(\bo r)=\{\table {q}/{4πε_0a^3}\bo r ,\text"," (r < a); {q}/{4πε_0r^3}\bo r ,\text"," (r ≥ a)$
Uniformly charged disk, radius $a$, charge $q$ (on axis, $z$) $\bo E(\bo z)={q}/{2πε_0a^2}z\({1}/{|z|}-{1}/{√{z^2+a^2}}\)$
Line charge, charge density $λ$ per unit length $\bo E(\bo r)={λ}/{2πε_0r^2}\bo r$
Electric dipole, moment $\bo p$ (spherical polar coordinates, $θ$ angle between $\bo p$ and $\bo r$) $\table E_r={p\cos θ}/{2πε_0r^3} ; E_θ={p\sin θ}/{4πε_0r^3}$
Charge sheet, surface density $σ$ $E={σ}/{2ε_0}$

$^a$For $ε_r=1$ in the surrounding medium.

Magnetic fields$^b$

Uniform infinite solenoid, current $I,n$ turns per unit length $B=\{\table μ_0nI ,\text", inside (axial)"; 0,\text", outside"$
Uniform cylinder of current $I$, radius $a$ $B(r)=\{\table {μ_0Ir}/{2πa^2} ,\text"," (r < a); {μ_0I}/{2πr} ,\text","(r ≥ a)$
Magnetic dipole, moment $\bo m$ ($θ$ angle between $\bo m$ and $\bo r$) $\table B_r=μ_0{m\cos θ}/{2πr^3} ; B_θ=μ_0{m\sin θ}/{4πr^3}$
Circular current loop of $N$ turns, radius $a$, along axis $z$ $B(z)={μ_0NI}/{2}{a^2}/{\(a^2+z^2\)^{3\text"/"2}}$
The axis $z$, of a straight solenoid, $n$ turns per unit length, current $I$ $B_{\text"axis"}={μ_0nI}/{2}\(\cos α_1-\cos α_2\)$

$^b$For $μ_r=1$ in the surrounding medium.

Potential field equations$^c$$\table {1}/{c^2}{∂^2φ}/{∂t^2}-∇^2φ={ρ}/{ε_0}; {1}/{c^2}{∂^2\bo A}/{∂t^2}-∇^2\bo A=μ_0\bo J$
